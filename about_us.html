About Us

At Wellspring Hospital, we are dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare with a commitment to quality, compassion, and innovation. With branches in Colombo, Kandy, and Galle, our mission is to deliver world-class medical services to individuals and families across Sri Lanka. We believe in a patient-centered approach, offering personalized care through a wide range of specialties, from general medicine to advanced surgical procedures. Our highly skilled medical professionals are equipped with state-of-the-art technology to ensure the best possible outcomes. At Wellspring, we prioritize not only treatment but also wellness, striving to create a healthier and happier community.
