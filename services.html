Services

Our hospital offers comprehensive services to meet all your healthcare needs. Our Emergency Care department is available 24/7 to provide immediate attention for acute illnesses and injuries. Our Surgical Services cover a wide range of procedures, from general surgery to specialized operations. We also offer advanced Diagnostic Imaging services, including X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans, to accurately diagnose and monitor health conditions. Additionally, our Maternity and Neonatal Care units provide exceptional care for mothers and newborns, ensuring a safe and supportive environment during and after childbirth.
